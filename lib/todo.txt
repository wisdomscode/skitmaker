Please brief description of the app.
It is a social app for making skits, more like a combination of Youtub and Tiktok.
1. Registered user can view skits, create both video skit and short skit
2. can Follow and be Followed
3. can like and unlike a skit
4. can share skits (video and short)
5  can download skit (video and short)
6. can comment on skits
7. view all users profile
8. update your profile (still working on this)
and some other feactures and some still to come
